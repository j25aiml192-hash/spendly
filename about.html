About Us

Our Mission:
SaaS Under Control

Team Bugg Slayers are building Spendly to help companies detect and eliminate unnecessary SaaS spending. We believe every company deserves clarity, control, and ruthless efficiency in their SaaS stack.

Built by Team Bugg Slayers

A small team of finance nerds, IT veterans, and engineering wizards obsessed with solving the SaaS waste problem. We've seen SaaS sprawl from every angle — and we're building the solution we wish we'd had years ago.

Our Principles

We believe in ruthless clarity

Every dollar should be visible, every decision should be easy.

Data over assumptions

Real spend data — from bank feeds, SSO logs, and accounting APIs — not surveys or self-reporting.

Systems are connected

Finance, IT, and HR data belong together. Fragmented tools create fragmented decisions — we fix that.

Progress is measurable

We track every dollar recovered, every license reclaimed, and every renewal caught — so ROI is never in doubt.
