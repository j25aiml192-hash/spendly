FAQ

Common questions

How long does setup take?
Most teams are fully connected in under 15 minutes. Spendly integrates directly with your bank feeds, SSO, and HR tools — no engineering required.
Can I switch plans later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated automatically.
Is my data secure?
Spendly is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit with AES-256 and TLS 1.3.
What integrations are supported?
We support 200+ integrations including Okta, Google Workspace, Slack, QuickBooks, Xero, BambooHR, Workday, and more. If we don't support yours, we'll build it.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes! The Starter plan is free forever for up to 10 tools. Standard plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.
