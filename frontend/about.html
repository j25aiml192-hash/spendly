Our Story

Born from frustration.
Built for clarity.

We've seen SaaS sprawl from every angle — finance teams drowning in invoices, IT managers chasing shadow subscriptions, and CFOs flying blind on software spend.

"We built the solution we wish we'd had years ago — one platform that connects the dots between finance, IT, and HR data."

Spendly was born at a hackathon, refined through real customer pain, and now saves companies millions in wasted SaaS spend every year.

Our Principles

We believe in ruthless clarity

Data over assumptions

Real spend data — from bank feeds, SSO logs, and accounting APIs — not surveys or self-reporting. Every recommendation is backed by evidence.

Systems are connected

Finance, IT, and HR data belong together. Fragmented tools create fragmented decisions — Spendly connects them all into one coherent picture.

Progress is measurable

We track every dollar recovered, every license reclaimed, every renewal caught early — so ROI is never in doubt. Your CFO will have a number to report.
